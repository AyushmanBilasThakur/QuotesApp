About the app

QuotesApp is an app where you can share your thoughts and quotes with no account and without even revealing your real name. This app is special as you won't need any user account to do anything in this app, each and every device uses its local storage to store some data and thus your computer becomes the local account for you. So enjoy sharing your thoughts anonymously with other people in this platform Quotesapp is a personal project of mine, and it is my first project intended to be in my portfolio. I'm hosting this site on github that means it is open source. I will provide the source link here.
